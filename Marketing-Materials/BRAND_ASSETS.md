# SANE/REBELS Brand Assets Guide

## Brand Identity

### The Name
**SANE/REBELS** - Two words, one slash, one mission.
- SANE = Building with intention, not chaos
- REBELS = Shipping despite fear, breaking free from the time-for-money trap
- The "/" = The balance, the duality, the meeting point

### Logo System

#### Primary Logo
The animated wordmark: `SANE/REBELS`
- SANE and REBELS alternate visibility
- They meet in the middle at purple
- Creates a breathing, living brand

#### Static Variations
1. **Full Logo (Light):** White text, purple slash, for dark backgrounds
2. **Full Logo (Dark):** Dark text, purple slash, for light backgrounds
3. **Icon Only:** S/R in purple on dark background
4. **Wordmark:** Text only, no icon

## Voice & Tone

### We Sound Like
- Warm, direct, curious
- Builder-energy (energized by making, not just advising)
- Confident but inviting (not cocky)
- A thoughtful partner who's done the work

### We Say
- "Machines do machine work. Humans stay human."
- "The rebellion? Going home on time."
- "The sanity? Being present when you get there."
- "Sane enough to build it. Rebel enough to ship it."

### We Don't Say
- Generic startup speak ("leverage", "synergy")
- Hustle porn ("grind", "crush it")
- Over-promising ("10x your revenue!")
- Cold/corporate jargon
- AI slop (no em-dashes, no "utilize")

---

## Visual Style

### Aesthetic
- Minimalist with revealed details on interaction
- Dark mode primary
- Subtle texture (grain overlay)
- Purposeful animation
- Clean whitespace

### Photography Style
- Authentic, not stock-feeling
- Natural lighting preferred
- People in flow states
- Warm, approachable tones
- Never overly polished or corporate

### Iconography
- Simple, line-based
- Consistent stroke width
- Rounded corners to match brand softness
- Monochrome with accent highlights
