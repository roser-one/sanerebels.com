# SANE/REBELS Slogans & Taglines

A collection of approved brand phrases for marketing use.

## Primary Tagline
**"We productize your service. You stay human."**
- Use for: Hero sections, main messaging, LinkedIn headline

---

## Philosophy Statements

### The Core
**"Machines got powerful. Time to be more human."**
- Use for: Opening statements, manifesto content

### The Rebellion
**"The rebellion? Going home on time."**
- Use for: Social posts, relatable content

### The Sanity
**"The sanity? Being present when you get there."**
- Use for: Follow-up to rebellion line, emotional content

### The Builder
**"Sane enough to build it. Rebel enough to ship it."**
- Use for: CTA areas, closing statements

## Usage Guidelines

1. **Consistency over creativity** - Use approved phrases, don't reinvent
2. **Punctuation matters** - Keep periods/question marks as written
3. **Context appropriate** - Match slogan weight to content weight
4. **Never modify** - The wordsmithing is intentional
